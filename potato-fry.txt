Ingredients:
 - 1 spoon oil
 - 3 potatoes
 - cumin seeds
 - salt
 - red chilli powder

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, add some oil
  2. cut potatoes into small wedges and add it to the saucepan with oil
  3. fry potatoes until they are golden in color
  4. Add cumin seeds, salt and red chilli powder and fry for 5 minutes
